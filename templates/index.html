About This Tool

This system uses deep learning (transfer learning with VGG16, ResNet50, and MobileNetV2) to classify car damage images into three severity levels. It was built to automate parts of the insurance claim review process.

Severity Levels

Minor

Small dents, scratches, or scuffs. Surface-level damage with no structural impact.

Moderate

Visible panel damage, broken lights, or bumper deformation. May require part replacement.

Severe

Structural damage, crumpled frame, or major deformation. Likely needs extensive repair.
